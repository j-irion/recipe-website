Tap Water

Description

This recipe creates a deliciously refreshing, low calorie, beverage with minimal preparation time. Even your kids will be able to make it!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 teaspoon water

Steps

  1. Place glass under tap.
  2. Turn tap on.
  3. Fill glass with water almost to top.
  4. Turn tap off.
  5. Top up glass with additional teaspoons of water until full.
  6. Use paper towel to mop up any spillage.
  7. Enjoy!
